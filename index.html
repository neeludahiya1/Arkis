Our products

It's great to create an LED product range that saves energy and costs less. But who will use it if the light it emits is harsh and unpleasant? We knew we could do better. That's why we've engineered LED product range that bring you the eco-friendly, budget-conscious lighting you want, with the warm, glowing hues we all crave. Arkis Lightings Pvt. Ltd. products range from LED bulbs, LED Spotlights, LED light strips, LED Lamps and others, thus providing effective and energy-efficient lighting in any environment. ARKIS Lights are fully integrated into the systematic structure of the overall lighting product range which also includes colorful lighting products. This gives lighting designers the freedom to use LED lighting tools and seamlessly combine them with conventional products, giving the users the benefit of using superior quality light products. ARKIS with its smart technology and cost-effective products transforms the base of conventional and CFL lighting to LED, a process which will help customer overall.

Why do we do this?

Our Products are designed with both optimum color rendering and energy efficiency in mind, so they always look as great as they work, whether you're in a living room, an office building, or an industrial warehouse. And with a full array of LED products to choose from, including new Industrial and Outdoor lighting options, you can bring the beauty of light to every room and surrounding - without breaking your budget in the process. The reason we can see color is actually because of light. Most objects reflect light to some degree; the amount of the wavelengths they absorb and reflect decide what light receptors in our eyes collect and transmit to our brains as colors. Therefore, the color given off by a light has enormous potential to affect the color of objects. The right light color can raise the aesthetic appeal of a residence to glorious new heights. The wrong light color can make things look dull.
