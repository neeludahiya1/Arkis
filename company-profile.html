Company Profile

Established in year 2017, Arkis Lightings Pvt. Ltd. strives to become a pioneer in providing international quality lightning solutions. Using high-end technology and smart innovation techniques, ARKIS provides LED lights for home, offices, industries, pharmaceutical, education and research institutes, healthcare facilities, retail spaces, transport infrastructure, road lighting and area lighting. Our product ranges from led bulbs, led spotlight, led light strips, led lamp and others. Arkis is ready to serve all your lighting needs. Where eco-friendly, dependable, versatile, budget conscious, low-maintenance lighting is essential – Arkis can meet your needs. Apart from this, Arkis is also in the business of trading, buying, selling, import and export of all kinds of lighting, LED lighting, Solar lighting, Electrical switches, electrical and electronic goods and also provide general and technical consultancy for the said related items. Besides, Arkis is doing the business of research and development, design, provision of technical assistance and licenses, consulting and otherwise of manufacturing systems including production, control and quality control and improvement of production lines relating to above objects.

Arkis Lightings Pvt. Ltd. is a technology Conscious Company focused on science-based lighting solutions. Drawing from its basic roots, the company has a track record of commercializing next-generation products targeted at the infrastructure, commercial, and residential markets. Its unique understanding of solid state lighting enables the company to capture the essence of light through minimalist designs that incorporate advanced feature sets. The materials and energy efficiencies achieved through this approach drive the competitive costing and, ultimately, successful products. Arkis Lightings Pvt. Ltd. Unique Understanding of the impact of light enables the company to bring unique products to market that improve the health & wellness of humans, plants and animals. It is our mission to be a key provider of LED lighting solutions to the marketplace.
